KeeMobile

KeeMobile password manager brings you top notch tool to access, create, and manage your passwords, documents and 2FA codes on the go.

Get for android

Using KeePass format in a password manager gives you control over where your data is stored and breaks free from proprietary cloud-only solutions. Store your files locally on your device for more security or synchronise automatically using popular cloud storage providers like Google Drive, pCloud, Sync (some other providers are available as read only).

Have an access across devices

KeePass files produced by KeeMobile are compatible with other KeePass password managers and can be opened on all of your devices.

Secure by design

Industry standard encryption using 256-bit AES with Argon2 KDF. Additional in-memory encryption.

More than just passwords

Create custom fields to securely store sensitive data like credit card numbers, IDs, and addresses. Protect your confidential files, photos, and documents as entry attachments.

Autofill

Quickly login using integrated autofill functionality with support for usernames and passwords.

Clean and intuitive user experience with support for legible light and dark themes.
